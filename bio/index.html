Biography

Dr. Junming DUAN is currently a tenure-track assistant professor at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen), joined in January 2026. He received his B.Sc. (2016) and Ph.D. (2021) degrees from Peking University, supervised by Prof. Huazhong Tang. From 2021 to 2023, he worked as a postdoctoral researcher at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), with Prof. Jan S. Hesthaven. From 2023 to 2025, he was a Humboldt Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Würzburg, with Prof. Christian Klingenberg. His research focuses on computational methods for problems arising in fluid dynamics, primarily involving high-order accurate numerical methods for hyperbolic partial differential equations, and efficient reduced-order modeling for parametrized time-dependent problems. He is supported by the Excellent Young Scientists Fund Program (Overseas). He has received several honors, including the Humboldt Research Fellowship for Postdocs, and the First Prize in Outstanding Youth Paper Award from the Beijing Society of Computational Mathematics.
